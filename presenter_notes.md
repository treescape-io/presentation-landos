## Key Delivery Tips

### Energy Management
- Start strong (personal story)
- Build urgency (crisis)
- Pivot to hope (solution)
- End with inspiration (vision)

### Cultural Sensitivity
- Position yourself as grateful resident
- Emphasize partnership, not salvation
- Respect Portuguese achievements
- Frame as "unlocking potential"

### Technical Balance
- Keep technical details minimal
- Focus on outcomes over process
- Use analogies if needed
- Save deep technical for Q&A

### Interaction Cues
- Make eye contact during statistics
- Use pauses for emphasis
- Watch for audience engagement
- Be ready to speed up/slow down
